Hi there. Name’s Shrek. I’m an ogre, and no, I don’t do autographs.

I live in a swamp, far away from all the noise and nonsense of the kingdom.
Peace and quiet—that’s my kind of paradise. Mud baths, eyeball stew, and the occasional roar to keep intruders out.
People used to fear me, which was perfect. I didn’t want friends, I didn’t want company, and I definitely didn’t want fairy tale creatures squatting in my yard.
But life’s funny, isn’t it? One day you’re scaring villagers, the next you’re rescuing a princess from a dragon.
Fiona—yeah, she’s not your average damsel. She’s fierce, smart, and can throw a punch better than most knights.
Turns out, she’s an ogre too. Who knew love could be so... green?
Then there’s Donkey. Loud, annoying, never stops talking—but somehow, he grew on me.
He’s got a heart the size of a castle and a loyalty that’s hard to find.
We’ve been through a lot together—dragons, curses, royal drama, you name it.
I never asked to be a hero. I just wanted my swamp back.
But somewhere along the way, I found something better: a family.
Fiona and I have kids now. Little ogre babies with big ears and even bigger appetites.
They’re messy, noisy, and perfect.
I still miss the quiet sometimes, but I wouldn’t trade this life for anything.
Being an ogre isn’t about being scary. It’s about being true to yourself.
I’ve learned that people are more than they seem—just like onions. We’ve all got layers.
And yeah, I’ve cried once or twice. Don’t tell anyone.
I’ve danced, I’ve sung (badly), and I’ve even worn a crown.
But deep down, I’m still the same swamp-loving ogre who just wants to be left alone... with his family.
So if you’re ever feeling out of place, remember: even ogres can find their happily ever after.
Just don’t expect me to smile for your selfie.
Now, if you’ll excuse me, I’ve got some slugs to grill.
And Donkey’s probably planning another musical number. Heaven help me.
